Uncle Chen Logistics

Freshness Delivered from Farm to City.

Uncle Chen Logistics is the heartbeat of Studio Umami’s supply network — connecting fresh Australian produce directly from local farms to restaurants across Sydney.

Every morning, our team sources seasonal vegetables, fruits, and key ingredients from trusted growers who share our commitment to quality. From farm gate to kitchen door, every step of the journey is temperature-controlled, time-tracked, and designed to preserve freshness.

We currently operate an extensive delivery network covering 48 shopping centres and food precincts across the Sydney metropolitan area, ensuring each partner receives their supplies on time, every time.

Our mission is simple:
to make freshness predictable, logistics seamless, and partnerships sustainable.
Because in every meal served, the first flavour that matters — is freshness.

What We Deliver

Farm-fresh vegetables and fruits from local Australian growers

Daily distribution to Sydney’s key retail and hospitality hubs

Cold-chain assurance and punctual delivery

Reliable service for restaurants, cafes, and quick-service brands

Our Values

Reliability — We deliver what we promise, every time.
Freshness — Quality starts at the farm and arrives intact at the table.
Partnership — We grow with our clients, one delivery at a time.
